Why Batching Pays

The more we group, the less each video costs.

The travel days on a trip get split across every seller filmed on it. The Southern California trip carries three sellers, so each of those videos lands around $1,800. A lone shoot in its own state has to carry the whole trip itself — closer to $2,400. Same quality either way; the difference is purely how many we can group together.

The one ask that saves money: a couple of sellers close a month off from their neighbors — Amarillo and Mebane, mainly. If those escrow dates can flex a week or two toward the rest of their region, Texas and the Southeast each collapse into a single trip instead of two — saving travel and roughly $1,200 in fees for the exact same twelve videos.
