Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer a free initial consultation?+
Yes. Every relationship starts with a free, no-pressure consultation where we learn about your situation and show you where we can help — and what it would cost. Book one anytime.
What size businesses do you work with?+
From solo entrepreneurs and 1099 contractors to growing companies that need controller and CFO-level support. We tailor our services to your stage — and grow with you.
Are you really a Dave Ramsey Endorsed Local Provider?+
We are. Clients frequently find us through Dave Ramsey's ELP program, and we hold ourselves to the standards of service and integrity that endorsement requires.
Do you do film and entertainment accounting?+
Yes — it's a specialty. We work with productions on Utah's State Motion Picture Incentive Program, handling production accounting, incentive maximization, and audit-ready reporting.
Do you offer services in Spanish?+
We do. Several team members are fluent in Spanish (and Portuguese), so we can serve you comfortably in your preferred language.
How do I share documents securely?+
Through our secure client portal, you can exchange documents, e-sign, and stay organized year-round. New clients can request access in seconds.
