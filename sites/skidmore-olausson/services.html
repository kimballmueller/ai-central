Tax Services

Pay what you owe — and not a dollar more

Proactive tax work for individuals, businesses, estates and nonprofits, handled by CPAs who plan ahead instead of just filing in April.

Tax Preparation

Accurate, thoroughly reviewed returns for individuals, corporations, partnerships, estates, trusts and nonprofits. We make sure every credit and deduction you're entitled to is captured — and that your return holds up to scrutiny.

  • Individual (1040)
  • Business & corporate
  • Pass-through entities
  • Estate & fiduciary
  • Nonprofit returns

Tax Planning

Tax is a year-round discipline, not a once-a-year event. We meet with you before deadlines hit to model scenarios, time income and deductions, and structure decisions so there are no surprises when it's time to file.

  • Year-end projections
  • Entity & compensation strategy
  • Retirement & investment timing
  • Multi-year planning

Tax Problems & IRS Resolution

Behind on returns? Received a notice? Facing back taxes or penalties? We step in, communicate with the taxing authorities on your behalf, and work toward the best resolution available for your situation.

  • Back taxes & unfiled returns
  • IRS & state notices
  • Penalty abatement
  • Payment arrangements

IRS Audit Representation

An audit notice is stressful — but you don't have to face it alone. We represent you from start to finish, manage the document requests and correspondence, and stand between you and the IRS so you can keep running your life and business.

  • Full representation
  • Documentation & response
  • Examination management

Business Services

The financial backbone for your business

Small-Business Accounting

Reliable bookkeeping, financial statements and general-ledger support that keep your records accurate and decision-ready. We help you understand the story behind the numbers so you can run a stronger business.

  • Bookkeeping
  • Financial statements

  • Software setup

QuickBooks & Accounting Software

Get your accounting software working for you. We assist with setup, clean-up, training and ongoing support in QuickBooks and Sage, plus help with general-ledger software so your books stay accurate between visits.

  • QuickBooks setup & training
  • Sage support
  • File clean-up
  • Ongoing assistance

Audits · Reviews · Compilations

The right level of assurance for your needs — from full audits to reviews and compilations. Our audit team brings deep expertise in financial-statement preparation and specialized construction accounting.

  • Financial-statement audits
  • Reviews
  • Compilations
  • Construction accounting

Strategic Business Planning & Consulting

A trusted advisor in your corner for the decisions that matter — entity selection, growth and succession planning, budgeting and the financial questions that keep owners up at night. We help you plan with confidence.

  • Entity selection

  • Growth & succession
