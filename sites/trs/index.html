Who We Are

A team you can trust — for decades, not seasons.

For over 95 combined years, the partners of Trader Orton & Spangler have provided quality, personalized tax, accounting, and consulting services to closely-held and family-owned businesses.

We now serve the third generation of some of our original clients — a continuity that reflects our dedication to high standards, seasoned tax professionals, and a relentless work ethic.

What We Do

Comprehensive tax, accounting & advisory services.

Two practices, one standard of care — from the return on your desk to the strategy behind your enterprise.

Tax & Accounting

  • Tax Preparation

    Individual and business returns across every entity type, estates and trusts, and multi-state work — prepared to make the process effortless and minimize your liability through careful planning.

  • Tax Representation

    Year-round monitoring of an ever-changing tax landscape, plus experienced representation before the IRS and state authorities when it matters most.

  • International Taxation

    Cross-border counsel for corporations with overseas operations and for U.S. residents working abroad or foreign citizens working in the U.S.

  • Cost Segregation Studies

    Identifying and separating personal-property assets to accelerate depreciation, improve cash flow, and reduce current tax burden.

Business Consulting

  • Entity Selection & Restructuring

    Your entity shapes your taxes and liabilities. We advise from inception through every stage of growth and transition.

  • Accounting Services

    Compilations and reviews, general ledger, bookkeeping, payroll, cash-flow analysis, and personal financial statements — clarity that surfaces opportunity.

  • CFO & Advisory Services

    A cost-effective way to add senior financial expertise: projections, M&A support, debt advising, and cash-flow strategy, virtual or in person.

  • Benefits, Pension & Retirement Plans

    Develop, implement, and administer benefit, retirement, and profit-sharing plans designed to maximize tax advantage.
